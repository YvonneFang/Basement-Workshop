Introduction

This timeline map is an effort to showcase Basement Workshop's history from 1970 to 1985 and its legacy, in rough chronological order, as well as in relation to the locations where key events happened. Basement Workshop is an important Asian American political and arts organization, established during the Asian American movement in the US. Its activities have impacted Asian American artists and activists around the country. Clicking on the different time periods and events on this interactive map will take you on a journey in New York's Chinatown and across the city in different historical moments, to explore the extensive reach of Basement Workshop's activism.
